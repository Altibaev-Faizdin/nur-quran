Sheikh Mishary Rashid Alafasy is one of the most renowned Quran reciters in the world. Born in Kuwait (1976), he is celebrated for his beautiful, melodious voice and deeply moving recitation style. He has memorized the entire Quran and has contributed enormously to Islamic music and recitation worldwide.
