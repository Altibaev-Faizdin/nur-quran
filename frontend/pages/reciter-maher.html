About This Reciter

Sheikh Maher Al Muaiqly is the Imam of the Grand Mosque (Masjid Al-Haram) in Makkah. His recitation is celebrated for its moving, heartfelt quality. He leads millions of worshippers in prayer every year and his recordings are among the most beloved in the world.
